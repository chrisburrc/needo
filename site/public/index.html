The product is a smartphone application that assists consumers in defining recurrent products (dry food, household) they don’t want to worry about and then scheduling the home delivery in an adaptive way.

Retailers want consumers to frequently go to the supermarket or visit their online shop to incite them to buy more as needed. Our service is disruptive because it releases people from this marketing dynamic that doesn’t add any value for them but mainly quits time to go to local grocery stores.
